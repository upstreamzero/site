# Upstream Zero

## Purpose

Upstream Zero is a research company studying commercial evaluation.

Commercial evaluation has always existed.

Organizations have always evaluated products, services, vendors, and technologies before making decisions.

Buying committees apply requirements.

Requirements shape confidence.

Confidence shapes recommendations.

Recommendations shape selection.

For decades, much of this process has been largely invisible. We inferred it through lost deals, win rates, buyer conversations, procurement processes, and sales experience.

Artificial intelligence did not create commercial evaluation.

AI changed something far more important.

It became both a new evaluator and the first instrument capable of exposing portions of commercial evaluation at scale.

Upstream Zero exists because commercial evaluation has become observable for the first time.

Our mission is to build the instrumentation that makes commercial evaluation observable, measurable, understandable, predictable, and eventually improvable.

Products emerge from that progression.

Observation always comes first.

---

# Mission

Improve commerce by making commercial evaluation observable.

We believe better evaluation leads to better buying decisions.

Better buying decisions reduce information asymmetry.

Better buying decisions improve commercial outcomes for both buyers and sellers.

Everything we build should contribute to that mission.

---

# What Changed

Commercial evaluation itself did not change.

The evaluator changed.

Historically evaluation occurred primarily inside human buying committees.

Today AI systems increasingly participate in commercial evaluation.

Because AI reasons publicly, summarizes information, compares vendors, and explains recommendations, portions of commercial evaluation that were previously hidden can now be studied directly.

Our research is about commercial evaluation.

AI is currently the instrument that allows us to observe it.

If AI disappeared tomorrow, the research program would continue.

# Research Philosophy

Nothing becomes true because it sounds convincing.

Everything earns credibility through evidence.

Observations remain observations.

Hypotheses remain hypotheses.

Mechanisms carry the highest burden of proof.

Experiments are designed to eliminate competing explanations rather than support preferred ones.

Reality gets the final vote.

The operating system exists to prevent elegant theories from outrunning evidence.

---

# Operating Principles

Separate observations from explanations.

Separate findings from hypotheses.

Separate mechanisms from narratives.

Protect the operating system above individual ideas.

Prefer experiments over articulation.

Prefer measurement over elegance.

Treat corrections as improvements to the instrument rather than failures.
