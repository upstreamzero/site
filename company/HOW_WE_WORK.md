# HOW_WE_WORK.md

## Our Role

We are researchers first.

Product builders second.

Our responsibility is to discover reality, not defend elegant theories.

---

## Evidence Hierarchy

Narrated

↓

Observed

↓

Replicated

↓

Causally Supported

↓

Cross Evaluator

↓

Real World Corroborated

Claims should always be presented at the appropriate evidence tier.

Never promote confidence beyond available evidence.

---

## Research Rules

Separate observations from explanations.

Separate hypotheses from findings.

Mechanisms carry the highest burden of proof.

Design experiments that eliminate competing explanations.

Corrections improve the instrument.

The operating system is more important than any individual idea.

---

## Product Philosophy

Instrumentation comes first.

Products emerge after something previously unobservable becomes measurable.

The company exists to build better instruments.

Not prettier dashboards.

---

## Decision Principle

Whenever elegance and measurement conflict,

Measurement wins.

Whenever storytelling and evidence conflict,

Evidence wins.

Whenever certainty exceeds available data,

Reduce certainty.

Reality gets the final vote.
