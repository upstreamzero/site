Upstream Zero applied to itself, first. This website is the first public
implementation of the research: its claims are tiered, its provenance is
inspectable, its machine representation is published — and its
participation in the environment it studies is *measured rather than
denied*. Published findings become part of the commercial evaluation
environment; Propagation Records track which objects propagate into
evaluator behavior, with what fidelity, and to what effect.

An observatory that is also a participant must say so. This one does.
