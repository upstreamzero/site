A vendor's candidacy has to survive a run of gates — getting surfaced,
passing screening, holding up in comparison, clearing validation, making
the final cut. And once a recommendation forms, it has to survive
paraphrase, re-sampling, model updates, and pointed follow-up questions.
How well a vendor survives all of that is its recommendation survivability.

We coined the term, and its precise, measurable definition is still being
worked out alongside M-1. We list it so the word is never doing
load-bearing work while it stays undefined.
