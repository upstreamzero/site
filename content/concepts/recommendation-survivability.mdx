title: Recommendation survivability

---

Working definition: the degree to which a vendor's candidacy survives the
successive gates of an evaluation process — surfacing, screening,
comparison, validation, final recommendation — and the degree to which a
recommendation, once formed, survives paraphrase, re-sampling, model
updates, and adversarial follow-up questions.

**Status honesty:** this is a founder-coined term whose precise,
measurable definition is still being developed alongside M-1. It is listed
so the vocabulary is never load-bearing while undefined.
