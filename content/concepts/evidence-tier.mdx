Every significant claim on this site carries a confidence rating on a
six-step scale: **Narrated → Observed → Replicated → Causally Supported →
Cross Evaluator → Real World Corroborated.** A claim is shown at its tier
and never above it.

Narrated is the label for something asserted but not yet demonstrated —
which describes most of this site's founding claims today. What each tier
requires is still being worked out in M-1. Until it is, nothing here rises
above Narrated.
