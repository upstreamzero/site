The six-step confidence scale every significant claim on this site carries:
**Narrated → Observed → Replicated → Causally Supported → Cross Evaluator →
Real World Corroborated.** A claim is always presented at its tier, and
confidence is never promoted beyond available evidence.

Narrated is not an insult — it is the honest label for anything asserted
but not yet demonstrated, including most of this site's own founding
claims. The tier system's operational definitions live in M-1, which is
under active development; until they exist, nothing here claims a tier
above Narrated.
