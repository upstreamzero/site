Upstream Zero is trying to build a category: the systematic study and
measurement of how organizations and AI systems evaluate commercial
offerings. Naming a category is a positioning act, so we hold this as a
program we are attempting, not an established fact about the world.

It is easy to mistake for SEO or AI-visibility work. The difference: those
sell better standing with evaluators; this studies the evaluation itself,
and the commercial work measures and diagnoses rather than promising any
evaluator behavior.
