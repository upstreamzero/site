The category Upstream Zero is attempting to build: the systematic study
and measurement of how organizations and AI systems evaluate commercial
offerings.

**Named honestly:** category creation is a positioning act, and this term
is presented as a program the company is attempting — not an established
fact about the world. It is *not* SEO, AEO, GEO, or AI-visibility
optimization: those sell improved standing with evaluators; this studies
evaluation itself, and its commercial work measures and diagnoses rather
than promises evaluator behavior (see the engagements' explicit
non-promises).
