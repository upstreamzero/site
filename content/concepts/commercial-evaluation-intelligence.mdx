We're trying to name and build a field: the study of how companies and AI
systems decide what they're going to buy. Calling it a category is a bet.
We treat it like one.

It gets mistaken for SEO or AI-visibility work. That work tries to improve
how evaluators see you. We study the evaluation itself. When we take on
commercial work, we measure and diagnose. We never promise to change what
an evaluator says about you.
