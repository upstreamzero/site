Every company is evaluated before it's selected. Buying committees apply
[requirements](/concepts/requirements), weigh the evidence, and form the
confidence that becomes a recommendation. That process is commercial
evaluation. It has always been enormous, and almost invisible — you could
see it only in its exhaust: win rates, lost deals, buyer anecdotes.

Commercial evaluation is the work we study. Requirements are the enduring
structure it observes.

Market research studies demand. Sales methodology studies persuasion. This
studies the decision itself.
