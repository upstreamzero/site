A company has two representations. One is for people: the website, the
marketing, the brand. The other is for machines — a structured account of
what the company actually does: the problems it solves, its capabilities,
which requirements it covers, its evidence and constraints. That second
one is its machine representation.

The website is only one rendering of a company's knowledge, not the
authoritative source. This site practices the distinction on itself: every
object also serves as JSON, the full graph is at `/graph.json`, and
Upstream Zero's own machine representation lives at `/company.json`.
