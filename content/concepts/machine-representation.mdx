The structured, machine-consumable account of what an organization
actually is and does: business problems solved, commercial capabilities,
requirement coverage, evidence, constraints, integrations, confidence. The
counterpart to the human representation (website, marketing, brand).

The website is one rendering of the company's knowledge; it is not the
canonical representation. This site practices the distinction on itself:
every object serves a JSON rendering, the full graph is exported at
`/graph.json`, and the company's own machine representation lives at
`/company.json`.
