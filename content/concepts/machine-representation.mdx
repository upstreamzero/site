Your company has two versions. One is written for people: the website, the
marketing, the brand. One is written for machines: a structured account of
what you actually do, the problems you solve, what you can back up, where
your limits are. Most companies only maintain the first.

That second version is your machine representation, and your website is
only one view of it. It isn't the source of truth. We hold ourselves to the
same standard. Every object here is also available as JSON, the full graph
is at `/graph.json`, and our own machine representation lives at
`/company.json`.
