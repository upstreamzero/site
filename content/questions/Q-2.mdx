Everything this observatory measures about AI evaluators generalizes to
"commercial evaluation" only through a bridge: resemblance, influence, or
replacement. These are three different research programs with three
different evidence requirements. The discipline's first job is testing its
own premise — which is why this question is written down before any
finding exists.
