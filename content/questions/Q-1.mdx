The founding bet of the research program, written as a question so it can
be tested instead of assumed. Commerce runs on representations: websites,
claims, demos, and now the machine-readable profiles AI evaluators read.
Does the *faithfulness* of those representations actually lead to better
selection outcomes? That's testable, and the company is staked on the same
answer. If fidelity doesn't pay, we got more than the research wrong.
