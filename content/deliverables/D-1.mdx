The artifact a diagnostic engagement produces. Contents specification:

- **Observation log** — what evaluators said and recommended, verbatim,
  instrument-stamped (evaluator, version, access mode, date)
- **Sampling conditions** — N, prompt variants, repeats, variance; printed
  in the report body, not an appendix
- **Requirement coverage read** — which category requirements your
  representations substantiate, fail to substantiate, or leave invisible
- **Confidence breakdown map** — where evaluator narration about you
  hedges, contradicts itself, or requests evidence
- **Declared limits** — what this report cannot tell you (mechanism;
  persistence across model updates)

**What it does not contain:** rankings promises, optimization tricks,
competitor disparagement, or any number we haven't earned the digits of.

**Example status:** none yet. When a real (consented, redacted) example
exists it will be linked here; an illustrative mock would be rendered in
the SPECIMEN register, never in the visual language of real findings.
