The fidelity-pays hypothesis. It is also the company's own honesty
condition: the commercial offering rests on the premise that understanding
and improving representation fidelity is worth paying for *because it
improves outcomes*. If this hypothesis fails, the research program loses
its commercial logic — and by our own rules, that result would be
published like any other.
