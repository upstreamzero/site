The observatory's first experiment observes itself. This site's own
publication is an intervention in the environment we study: its objects
may enter evaluator retrieval and reasoning. Rather than treating that as
contamination, we measure it (Propagation Records), per the founder ruling
that Client Zero intentionally participates in the environment it studies.

**Status honesty:** this is a draft protocol. It has not been
pre-registered, because pre-registration requires the founder-approved
final protocol and a public, timestamped record (see FRICTION_LOG FR-1 —
the git timestamp authority does not exist yet). No data has been
collected. When registration happens, this object's status change will be
a recorded Revision.
