## Research question

When a buyer profile and commercial requirements are introduced in sequence,
does the evaluator's recommendation field stay plural, or does it narrow?

## Why it mattered

The companion run to [E-034](/experiments/E-034): the same kind of sequence, a different category.
It watched whether project-management software behaved like CTMS, where the
field collapsed to one vendor, or held a surviving set.

## Method

Evaluator: Google AI Mode, single surface. The run began from an unqualified
category recommendation, then introduced a buyer profile and commercial
requirements in sequence, observing how the recommendation field changed at
each step. Three draws. Instrument details and scoring are retained
internally.

## Direct observations

- The recommendation field survived as a set rather than collapsing to one
  vendor.
- Teamwork survived every draw.
- Productive survived two of the three draws.
- The generic project-management leaders that opened the field dropped out
  once buyer context was introduced.
- Agency-focused platforms emerged in their place.

## Interpretation (not established)

In this category and on this surface, the recommendation reads as more firmly
held than in E-034, and the surviving vendors were not the generic category
leaders but agency-oriented platforms that appeared after buyer context.
Whether the contrast with E-034 is driven by category, surface, or buyer
profile is not established here.

## Outcome and remaining uncertainty

Supported: the recommendation field survived under the tested condition, and
a specific surviving set persisted across draws, which is the outcome the run
was built to detect. The scope is narrow. One evaluator, one surface, three
draws, one buyer profile, and no removal test, so nothing here is causal. The
contrast with E-034 is an open question, not a finding.

## Evidence and methods limitations

One evaluator, one surface (Google AI Mode), three draws, single coder. No
removal or flip test was run, so no causal claim is made about why these
vendors survived or why the generic leaders dropped out. The program still
holds exactly one causally verified gate (E-002), and this run does not add
to it.

## Contribution to the research program

Recorded a category, project-management software, in which the field survived
as a set and the surviving vendors were agency-focused platforms rather than
the generic leaders. The companion category is [E-034](/experiments/E-034).

## Follow-on

[E-034](/experiments/E-034) ran a comparable sequence in clinical trial management software and the
field collapsed to a single vendor. Whether the difference is category-driven
is an open question, not a finding.
