M-1 is the most important document we will publish: the
working definitions of the six evidence tiers. What qualifies a claim as
**Narrated**, **Observed**, **Replicated**, **Causally Supported**,
**Cross Evaluator**, or **Real World Corroborated**. How many runs
constitute replication. Which evaluators count as independent. What counts
as real-world corroboration.

The tier *names* and their order are settled. The thresholds are not. They
are a founder decision, under active development, and this page will hold
the versioned definitions when they exist. Until then, every claim on this
site is presented at Narrated, which requires no definition beyond honesty.
