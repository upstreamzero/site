The founding claim, presented at its honest tier. AI systems now
participate in commercial evaluation, and unlike human buying committees,
their evaluation behavior can be sampled, recorded, and experimented on at
scale. What is observable is the *evaluators'* behavior — human committees
remain as opaque as ever; generalization runs through the bridge
hypothesis (H-1).

This claim will rise through the tiers only as published observations and
experiments support it. Until then it is what it says on the label:
Narrated.
