title: Representation and evidence gap analysis
created: "2026-07-13"
status: offered with declared uncertainty
maturity: experimental
---

Systematic comparison of what an organization actually does against what
its representations, human and machine-legible, allow an evaluator to
verify: which requirements go unsubstantiated, which claims carry no
checkable evidence, and which capabilities are invisible to a machine
reader.

**Why this is marked experimental:** the analysis framework derives from
the research program's untested hypotheses (H-2 most directly) and no
published method yet backs it. It is diagnostic work performed carefully
and labeled honestly.

**What this cannot tell you:** that closing the gaps will change any
particular evaluator's recommendation. We measure and diagnose; we do not
promise evaluator behavior.
