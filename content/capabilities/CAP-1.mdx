Structured observation of how AI evaluators surface, compare, validate,
and eliminate a specific organization in matched buying scenarios: what is
said, what is recommended, what evidence is requested, and where
confidence visibly breaks down.

**Why this is marked experimental:** a capability cannot be marked
operational until it derives from published, stable method (the build
enforces this). M-1 is under active development and no observation method
has yet been published, so this capability is offered as what it is:
careful, honest, early-instrument work, with sampling conditions and
variance disclosed, not hidden.

**What this cannot tell you:** why the evaluator behaves as it does
(mechanism), or whether its behavior will survive the next model update
(see Q-3).
